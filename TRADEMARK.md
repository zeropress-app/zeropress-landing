# ZeroPress Trademark Policy

"ZeroPress", the ZeroPress logo, and related brand assets are trademarks of the ZeroPress Project.

This document defines how the ZeroPress trademark may be used. It is intended to protect users from confusion and to preserve the integrity of the ZeroPress brand. This policy applies independently of the software license.

---

## Allowed Uses (No Permission Required)

You may use the ZeroPress name **without prior permission** in the following contexts, provided that the usage is accurate and does not imply endorsement or official status:

- Statements of compatibility (e.g., "Plugin for ZeroPress", "Theme compatible with ZeroPress")
- Descriptive or factual references (e.g., "Built using ZeroPress", "Powered by ZeroPress")
- Educational, editorial, or review content (blog posts, documentation, tutorials, videos, training materials)

### Examples (Allowed)
- `MyTheme for ZeroPress`
- `Guide: Deploying ZeroPress on Cloudflare`
- `Migrating from WordPress to ZeroPress`

---

## Uses Requiring Prior Written Permission

The following uses require **explicit written approval** from the ZeroPress Project:

- Using the ZeroPress name or logo as part of a **product name, service name, company name, or domain name**
- Using the ZeroPress trademark in marketing or branding in a way that could reasonably imply **official affiliation, sponsorship, certification, or endorsement**
- Offering a hosted, managed, or commercial service branded as an official ZeroPress offering

### Examples (Approval Required)
- `ZeroPressCloud.com`
- `ZeroPress Hosting`
- `ZeroPress Pro`, `ZeroPress Enterprise`, `ZeroPress Edition`
- `Official ZeroPress Marketplace`

---

## Prohibited Uses

The following uses are **not permitted under any circumstances**:

- Claiming or implying partnership, endorsement, or official status without authorization
- Distributing modified versions of the software under the name “ZeroPress”
- Registering trademarks, domains, or social media accounts that are confusingly similar to ZeroPress
- Using the ZeroPress logo or brand assets in a misleading, deceptive, or harmful manner

---

## Open-Source Forks and Derivative Works

- Forks and derivative works **must use a distinct name and branding**
- References to ZeroPress must be purely descriptive (e.g., "Forked from ZeroPress")
- Forks must not present themselves as the original or official ZeroPress project
